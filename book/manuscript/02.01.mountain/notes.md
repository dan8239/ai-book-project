	- About 30M diameter

  - Diamond edges on the drill bits have been removed by looters a few hundred years back

  - Enormous rotating disc on the mouth end
  - It has precision drill cores for injecting precision explosives in the event that it was more energy efficient to blast things that way

## The Entrance

### The Fissure

### The Eye of the Needle

- Second obstacle - have to **crawl under** something
- Ramona calls it "the camel's hump" or references the proverb

**The Proverb Exchange:**
- "Easier for a camel to pass through the eye of a needle than a rich man to enter heaven"
- Sam: "Why would I know that?"
- Ramona explains: It's not really about rich people
- Jerusalem had a gate called "the Eye of the Needle"
- Camels had to go on their knees to get through
- They had to humble themselves and crawl
- Sam: "I've never heard the proverb, why would I know the subtext?"
- Ramona: "It's not a proverb, it's... heathens. Freaking atheists."
- Teasing, loving exchange

### The Second Subchamber

- After crawling through, they're in a second chamber
- Within that: the root cellar entrance

### The Root Cellar Door

- 30-degree angle, into hillside
- Concrete, old rusted-looking door
- Locks on it, looks decrepit, like it could never work
- Appears impossible to unlock

### The Reveal

- Ramona's biometrics unlock it
- When doors swing open: **well-oiled, noiseless**
- The rust/decay was camouflage
- Underneath an overhang - can't be seen from satellite
