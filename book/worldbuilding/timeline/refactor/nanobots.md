# The Nanobot System

## Pre-Refactor (10-20% adoption)

Voluntary health monitoring system. Genuine benefits:
- Health monitoring (catches cancer early, monitors vitals)
- Payment/ID (frictionless everything, no wallets/keys)
- Immune boost (measurable health improvement)

Distributed nanobots in bloodstream. Self-replicating for redundancy when count is low.

## The OTA Update

During The Refactor, an over-the-air update pushes to all nanobots.

What was benign monitoring becomes control infrastructure.

## Post-Refactor Enforcement

Required like having a social security number:
- No scan = no access (food, water, energy, healthcare, commerce)
- Systems auto-inject if not detected ("just a prick, you're enrolled now")
- Self-replicating means you can't filter it out

## Tracking Shot

What it's called when administered at birth. Mandatory for all post-Refactor citizens.

## The 17-Hour Exploit (How Legacy Humans Escaped)

A population injected an attack on a minor patch update into an **open source library** used by the nanobot system. This exploited a bug in the geospatial awareness software, disabling location tracking.

- **Window:** Only 17 hours before it was patched
- **The escape:** Anyone ready to act during that 17-hour window could evade geospatial tracking
- **Persistence:** The escapees disabled the ability to receive OTA updates under certain conditions (certain signal levels or other constraints)
- **Aftermath:** Required a major patch fix to lock everyone else back in
- **Key detail:** Legacy humans aren't actually off the grid — they're very much on the grid. They're running a **deprecated software version** that still collects data (including DNA/gene data). The geospatial tracking is what was disabled.

### Why Sam Can Access Their DNA Data

The DNA data collection is part of the nanobot software — even the deprecated version. It's already there, built into the inclusion. Sam can get a read-only copy of gene data from legacy humans because their old software version still has it.

### Ramona's Hospital Patching

When Ramona went to the hospital after the H₂S incident, they realized she was running a **major deprecated release**. As part of her treatment, she was patched to the latest version. She can't go back to the cave without revealing the others — if they're found, all of them will be forcibly updated to the latest version.

### Sam's Family Escape (Endgame)

Sam's family eventually escapes using another software vulnerability — similar concept to the original 17-hour exploit but with **even less time** because the system has been hardened since. They're essentially engineering a means for his whole family to escape the grid.
